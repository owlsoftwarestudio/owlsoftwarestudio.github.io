# PriceTrack Support

PriceTrack is a grocery price tracking app that helps you scan receipts, add purchases manually, and compare grocery prices over time.

## Support Contact

**Developer:** Owl Software Studio

**Support Email:** support@owlsoftware.studio

**App Name:** PriceTrack - Grocery

## Common Help Topics

### Receipt Scanning
- Grant camera access when prompted
- Make sure the receipt is well lit and fully visible
- If AI receipt parsing fails, you can still enter items manually

### Household Sync
- Sync is optional and only turns on after you create or join a household
- You can disable sync from the app if you no longer want cross-device sharing

### Purchases and Price Tracking
- Add items manually if you do not want to scan a receipt
- Review past purchases to track price changes across stores
- Use the product history views to compare trends over time

### Permissions
- Camera access is needed for scanning receipts
- Photo library access is needed if you want to choose a receipt image from your device

## Data and Privacy Help

PriceTrack stores data locally by default. If you enable household sync, your data may also be synced across your devices. If you have questions about your data, privacy, or deletion requests, contact support using the email above.

## Before Contacting Support

Please include:
- Your device model
- Your iOS version
- A short description of the issue
- Screenshots if relevant
- Whether the issue happens during receipt scanning, purchase entry, or sync
